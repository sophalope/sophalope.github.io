about

sophie shiff (they/she) is an artist and educator based in covington, ky. their primary mediums are drawing, ceramics, and puppetry. when they're not making art, you can find them riding bikes with their friends, spending time with their backyard chickens, and advocating for affordable housing and pedestrian infrastructure.
