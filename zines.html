MICA DIVEST ZINE > 2019

This was a small, 8.5 x 11, one page zine that I made to inform Maryland Institute College of Art (MICA) students about divestment from fossil fuels. This was in coordination with an action put on by MICA Organizers and Activists and Students of Sustainability clubs to inform students about divestment and to get letters and drawings to present to the Board of Trustees.
